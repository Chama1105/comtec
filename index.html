Who We Are..?

Comtec Systems Group is a major holding company in Sri Lanka which provides services in a vast area with it’s outstanding subsidiaries. Committed to igniting growth through engineering, we at Comtec Systems Group constantly strive to offer cutting-edge engineering products, service and other services that can facilitate the development of efficient and reliable infrastructure to our discerning customers who value quality and reliability of such facilities in order to stay ahead of their competition.
 
Our vision is to Be the preferred service provider in infrastructure solutions in Sri Lanka by extending our technolo- gy leadership into core areas of engineering, spanning civil, hydropower, telecommunications, waterways, structural and built environment whilst creating new solutions that can meet tomorrow’s challenges.
 
Our mission is that We will endeavour to surpass the expectations of our valued customers by ensuring an end-to-end service delivery in all projects undertaken by us via meeting their unique needs and challenges through a process of efficient, flexible, reliable and economical engineering solutions.

The Leadership

S.A.A.R.SUBASINGHE

Managing Director

Mr. Ajith Subasinghe, Chairman & Managing Director of Comtec Systems Group is accomplished with 30 years of Engineering experience, who transformed himself into an extremely successful entrepreneur and philanthropist, contributing his wealth and expertise for the well-being of the society.
 
He is a professional management specialist who has built successful enterprises, including joint venture businesses with partners of international repute and leads Comtec Systems (Pvt) Ltd., an ISO 9001/2008 certified Company operating in the sphere of telecommunications. He also heads seven of the company’s subsidiaries.
 
Mr. Subasinghe has also worked in Japan for nearly 3 years and has received extensive training in Japan on telecommunication technology with a leading Japanese Conglomerate. He has been conferred with Deshamanya, Deshashakthi, Deshabandhu and Lankaputhra honorary titles for his invaluable contribution to the Nation and the people of Sri Lanka.

Comtec Systems

(PVT) LTD.

Comtec Systems (Pvt) Ltd is the brainchild of an energetic and innovative group of engineers whose expertise ranged from construction, hydropower and telecommunications to waterways, structural and built environment solutions. The Company which was incorporated in 1999 has since made giant strides in the Sri Lankan engineering arena, rapidly growing beyond its initial expectations to become a premier provider of high-end telecommunication infrastructure and cabling services. The Company has also made its presence felt in the civil engineering, renewable energy, road construction and related services with a range of successfully concluded projects to its credit.
 
Driven by a passion to ignite growth through innovative infrastructure solutions, Comtec Systems (Pvt) Ltd. has carved out a niche for itself as a preeminent service provider in infrastructure solutions, specializing in high quality aluminum, copper and fibre optic cable and systems solutions for the energy, construction, industrial and communications sectors.

RESORT (PVT) LTD.
